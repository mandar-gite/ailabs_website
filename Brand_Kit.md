* ### 4. Logo Usage Guidelines To maintain visual integrity, please adhere to these guidelines: 
* **Minimum Size:** To ensure readability and detail, the logo should never be reproduced smaller than 1.5 inches (or approximately 144 pixels wide for digital use). 
* **Clear Space (Exclusion Zone):** Always maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to at least 1/4 of the logo's height. No other graphic elements or text should intrude into this space. 
* **Color Variations:** * 
* **Full Color:** Use the provided full-color logo on cream/off-white or light backgrounds.
* **Monochromatic (Dark):** For dark backgrounds, a white or light-gold version of the logo might be necessary for contrast. 
* **Monochromatic (Light):** On light backgrounds, a dark blue or black version can be used. 
* **Forbidden Modifications:** * Do not stretch or distort the logo. * Do not change the colors of the logo (except for approved monochromatic versions). * Do not add filters, drop shadows, or other effects. * Do not rearrange or alter the elements within the logo. * Do not place the logo on busy or clashing backgrounds that impair readability.
